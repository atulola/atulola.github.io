National Flag

The tricolour flag represents courage, peace and prosperity.

The Indian Peacock symbolizes grace and beauty.

The Lotus symbolizes purity and enlightenment.

National Tree

The Banyan tree symbolizes longevity.

National Fruit

The Mango is known as the king of fruits.

National River

The Ganga is India's most sacred river.
